Sponsorship

The Tampa Bay Chapter partners with sponsors to help ensure that security awareness continues to be a high priority. Our goal is to build lasting relationships with our sponsors by providing products, services, and education benefiting ISC2 members and chapter constituents.

Why Sponsor?

Sponsoring the ISC2 Tampa Bay Chapter strengthens your name recognition, increases brand awareness, and provides enhanced visibility within civil, commercial, and national security sectors. These targeted marketing opportunities attract new business and solidify existing relationships.

The Tampa Bay Chapter is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Contributions may be tax-deductible (consult your tax advisor).

Sponsorship Benefits — $500 Annual Fee

  • Logo placement with hyperlink on sponsorship webpage (alphabetically listed)
  • Logo inclusion in chapter newsletters
  • Receipt of all chapter newsletters and mailings
  • Listing with hyperlink in chapter emails to membership
  • Sponsor recognition by name at each event
  • Opportunity to suggest topics and speakers
  • Two complimentary guest passes per chapter meeting
  • Product/service displays during meeting breaks
  • Thank you gift presented at a chapter meeting

Contact

For sponsorship inquiries, contact chapter Treasurer David Chen at: d.chen@isc2chapter-tampabay.org
